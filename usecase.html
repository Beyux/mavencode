FINANCE

At its core the financial industry has always been driven by data in the form of transaction ranges

Value

Simple exchange of money or credit in a store for goods, all the way to multi-million dollar automated stock trades that execute in mere fractions of a second. Given that banking institutions are in the business of both warehousing (holding) and trading risk the opportunity for advanced data analytics to gain a competitive advantage in the financial industry is enormous.

Illustration

AI & ML modeling can be used to more accurately assess the default probabilities on a portfolio of loans, predict whether or not a given credit card transaction is fraudulent, or even predict the price of securities themselves (stocks, bonds, futures, etc.) leading to new automated trading strategies.

Solution

At MavenCode, we leverage Google Cloud technology products like BigQuery, Cloud Bigtable, Pubsub, and Cloud ML to build scalable, end to end data pipelines that can be easily tuned to ingest anything from large batch jobs to high-frequency market data. The data pipelines enable us to work together with our clients to develop advanced analytical solutions that provide them with an edge in the hyper-competitive financial market place.

Oil & Gas

Machine learning (ML) is transforming the ways in which oil and gas exploration is done. With ML techniques, researchers, geoscientists and reservoir engineers can leverage insights from advanced deep learning models to identify complex patterns such as faults or stratigraphic geometries from seismic datasets in a sophisticated ML-based workflow for multiple iterations and therefore accelerating the whole process. By leveraging on Google Cloud technology our team is actively working on data-pipelines optimized to orchestrate this kind of high capacity workflow that enhances collaboration between data scientist, geoscientist and every stakeholder in the organization.
