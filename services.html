Data Preparation & Feature Engineering

Data is the lifeblood that drives any successful AI & ML business strategy, but most times, data collected is not ready for analysis just yet. The data needs to be cleaned, transformed and prepared in a format that suits the ML model. Furthermore,contextual understanding of the data is an invaluable part of the process.

We begin to develop this strategy for clients by closely assisting with data gathering, cleaning,transformation, and preparation

Once data is available in data lakes or data warehouses, we then work side by side with the client and domain experts to develop a deeper understanding of the data.

Following this rigorous process allows us to understand and extract key features of the data, a process more formally known as Feature Engineering.

Once completed, transformation is performed on the data before it is fed into the ML model.

Data Visualization

Storytelling with data is an integral part of any successful data acquisition, analysis, and monetization strategy. Visualizations bring data to life and shed light on the “why” question in decision making.

Mavencode comes in by helping clients translate complex and massive data into actionable insights with visualizations.

We also offer consultations on how to use those insights to stay ahead of the curve

AI & ML Algorithm Implementation

Implementing the appropriate Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence algorithms to your problem impacts the results you get in the long run. MavenCode assists with choosing the right AI or ML algorithm for your immediate need, and helps guide the client through the entire training, testing and validation process

A clear definition of the problem statement is the first step in determining what Machine learning algorithms to be used.

We understand that our clients; challenges can be very specific and so they require a unique approach. Our professionals walk you through the selected model before validation.

Training and testing is done with different measures of validation on the data, depending on what ML or AI model we implemented

Finally, our consultants work with client developers and data scientists to put the AI & ML model into production so it can be used for prediction and support business operations.

Data Pipeline

Data Pipelines help automate the processes involved in extracting, transforming, combining, validating, and loading data for further analysis and visualization.

At MavenCode, we build scalable AI & ML data pipelines for organizations interested in monetizing the ever increasing amounts of data generated by their customers, products (e.g. IoT devices), or services.

These pipelines easily adapt to data of any frequency and can scale up and down easily as the velocity of data increases or decreases.

Engineered to be robust and portable end to end solutions, MavenCode’s data pipelines enable large scale AI & ML modeling for data scientists and data engineers in an organization, helping to cut down the time needed to productionize a model, and therefore increasing overall data strategy ROI.
